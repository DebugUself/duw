问题在 `参差` 的行为是无法形成团队的

	Coming together is a beginning;
	Keeping together is progress;
	Working together is success!

\<--- [Henry Ford][23]

- 所以, 有了 大妈 随见随怼的持续嗯哼...
- 但是, 想象一年后, 回想几十周前自己作的那些 `图样图森破`
- 却没现成的资料来出示给后进来嗯哼?
- 不科学, 值得记录的, 就应当有个形式固定下来

What is DUW?
Why we make DUW?
What are the possibilities of DUW?

	Coming together is a beginning;
	Keeping together is progress;
	Working together is success!

\<--- [Henry Ford][24]

What a pity!
